Quranic Principles
==================

The Quranic Principles module is the foundation of AQI. It captures the mathematical codes, scientific references, hidden patterns, literary devices, and ontological hierarchy embedded in the Quran.

Mathematical Codes
------------------

The Quran contains intricate mathematical patterns that serve as a divine signature:

- **19-Based System**: The Quran has 114 surahs (19 x 6). The number 19 appears as a guardian over Hell (74:30).
- **Word Symmetries**: Paired concepts appear equal times:
  - *dunya* (world) = *akhirah* (hereafter) = 115 times each
  - *malaika* (angels) = *shayateen* (devils) = 88 times each
  - *hayat* (life) = *mawt* (death) = 145 times each
  - *salah* (prayer) = 5 times daily, mentioned 5 times in key contexts
- **Sea/Land Ratio**: The word *bahr* (sea) appears 32 times, *barr* (land) 13 times. Ratio: 32/(32+13) = 71.11% sea, 28.89% land — matching Earth's actual water/land coverage with 99.9% accuracy.
- **Golden Ratio**: Structural proportions in key surahs approximate phi (1.618).
- **Prime Patterns**: Many key counts are prime numbers (19, 114, 286 ayahs in Al-Baqarah).

Scientific References
---------------------

The Quran contains descriptions of natural phenomena confirmed by modern science:

- **Embryology** (23:14): Six stages — nutfah, alaqah, mudghah, bones, flesh, new creation.
- **Big Bang** (21:30): "The heavens and earth were joined, then We separated them."
- **Expanding Universe** (51:47): "We built the universe with power, and We are expanding it."
- **Mountains as Pegs** (78:6-7): Mountains have deep roots, stabilizing the crust.
- **Iron from Space** (57:25): "We sent down iron" — not formed on Earth.
- **Pain Receptors** (4:56): Skin replacement and pain — nerves located in skin.
- **Frontal Lobe** (96:15-16): "Lying, sinning forelock" — prefrontal cortex controls decision-making.

Hidden Patterns
---------------

- **Abjad Numerals**: Each Arabic letter has a numerical value used in traditional Quranic analysis.
- **Muqattatat**: 29 surahs begin with disconnected letters (Alif-Lam-Mim, Ha-Mim, etc.) in 11 distinct groups.
- **Ring Composition**: Many surahs exhibit chiastic structure where the beginning mirrors the end.
- **Structural Symmetry**: The Quran is divided into two equal halves at Surah Al-Kahf (18:19).

Literary Devices
----------------

- **Metaphors**: The Light Verse (24:35) contains 7 layers of metaphor in a single ayah.
- **Parables**: 31 parables throughout the Quran, each teaching profound lessons.
- **Oaths**: Allah swears by natural phenomena (sun, moon, fig, olive, time).
- **Rhetorical Questions**: Used to provoke reflection and self-examination.
- **Repetition Patterns**: Key phrases repeated exactly 31 times across the Quran.

Ontological Hierarchy
---------------------

AQI models the complete hierarchy of existence from the Quran and Sunnah:

- **Level 0**: Allah — Wajib al-Wujud (Necessary Existence)
- **Level 1**: Arsh (Throne) — Greatest creation
- **Level 2**: Kursi (Footstool) — Vast beyond heavens and earth
- **Level 3**: Malaikat (Angels) — Created from light, no free will
- **Level 4**: Jin — Created from smokeless fire, have free will
- **Level 5**: Insan (Humans) — Created from clay, have free will + 'aql
- **Level 6**: Hayawan (Animals) — Have sensation, limited cognition
- **Level 7**: Nabat (Plants) — Grow, reproduce, respond to environment
- **Level 8**: Jamad (Inanimate) — No life, no sensation

Each level has distinct properties: existence type, knowledge capacity, free will, and epistemic access.
